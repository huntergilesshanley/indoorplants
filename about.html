I created this project in 2007 when I was first given an indoor plant by my mother. Since then, I haven't looked back. I wanted to bring some education about houseplants to the people.

Up until 2008, I could see only 1-2 new houseplants come into my house every few months and more would die than live.

I started writing about how to care for and plant and keep houseplants healthy.

My mission is to share knowledge about houseplants and give these amazing, beautiful and unusual plants a voice.

Let's change the perception of houseplants from boring, unsightly "green things" to enjoyable plants that will help us live well and live longer.

As an instructor and writer, I hope that you will help me inspire you to grow and care for beautiful plants of all kinds.
